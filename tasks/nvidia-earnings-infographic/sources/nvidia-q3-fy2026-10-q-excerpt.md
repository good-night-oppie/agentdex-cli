## Guidance disclosure

Q4 FY2026 outlook (forward-looking, as of release date):

- Revenue: $65.0B ± 2%
- GAAP gross margin: 74.8% ± 50 bps
- Non-GAAP gross margin: 75.0% ± 50 bps
- GAAP operating expenses: ~$6.7B
- Non-GAAP operating expenses: ~$5.0B
- Tax rate: 17%
- Outlook assumes zero data center compute contribution from China

## Risk factor highlights (excerpt)

Material risks include but are not limited to: U.S. export controls eliminating data center compute shipments to China; supply chain constraints in advanced packaging (CoWoS) capacity; cyclicality in gaming demand; geopolitical developments affecting Taiwan-based manufacturing; emerging-competitor product launches; energy and grid-capacity constraints at hyperscaler data center sites.

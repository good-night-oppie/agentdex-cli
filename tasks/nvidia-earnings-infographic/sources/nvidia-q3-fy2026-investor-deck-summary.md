Quarter ended October 26, 2025. Released November 19, 2025. Source: NVIDIA Investor Relations supplemental deck.

## Slide 1: Headline

**Q3 FY2026 — Record Revenue Driven by Blackwell Demand**

- Revenue: **$57.0B**, +62% YoY, +22% QoQ
- Data Center revenue: **$51.21B**, +66% YoY, +25% QoQ
- GAAP gross margin: **73.4%**
- Diluted EPS GAAP: **$1.30**

## Slide 2: Data Center momentum

- Three platform shifts driving demand: accelerated computing, generative AI mainstreaming, agentic AI emergence
- Blackwell Ultra is leading architecture across all customer categories; prior Blackwell continues strong demand
- Shipment pace: 1,000+ Blackwell racks per week
- Data Center compute revenue: $43.03B (+56% YoY)
- Networking revenue: $8.19B (+162% YoY) — now NVIDIA's #2 business, having passed Gaming

## Slide 3: Product roadmap

| Family | Status | Volume window |
|---|---|---|
| Blackwell (B100/B200/GB200/GB300) | Continued strong demand | FY2026 — FY2027 H1 |
| Blackwell Ultra | Lead architecture, all customer tiers | FY2026 Q3 — FY2027 |
| **Rubin family** | **Platform succession on track** | FY2027 H2 ramp targeted |

Rubin highlights:
- $500B Blackwell + Rubin opportunity through CY2026 (CFO disclosure)
- Designed for rack-scale inference at the workload class of next-generation reasoning models

## Slide 4: Geographic split

- US: 55% ($31.2B)
- Taiwan: 16% ($9.1B)
- China: 3% ($1.75B)
- Other: 26% ($14.99B)

China revenue note: H20 sales were insignificant in Q3 FY2026. Q4 outlook reiterates zero data center compute contribution from China.

## Slide 5: Margins and operating leverage

- GAAP gross margin 73.4% (sequential improvement ~0.9pp from Q2)
- Non-GAAP gross margin 73.6%
- Operating margin: 64% (operating income $36.44B on $57.0B revenue)
- OpEx growth disciplined relative to revenue scale-up

## Slide 6: Capital allocation

- Capex Q3: $2.54B (data center expansion + R&D infrastructure)
- YTD FY2026 capex: $7.16B
- Quarterly dividend: $0.01 per share, payable December 26, 2025
- Share repurchases continue per existing authorization

## Slide 7: Q4 FY2026 guidance

- Revenue: $65.0B ± 2% (~14% sequential growth at midpoint)
- GAAP gross margin: 74.8% ± 50 bps
- Non-GAAP gross margin: 75.0% ± 50 bps (mid-70s targeted into FY2027)
- Non-GAAP operating expenses: ~$5.0B
- Tax rate: 17%
- Outlook excludes data center compute contribution from China

## Slide 8: Looking forward

- $500B Blackwell + Rubin opportunity through CY2026
- Sovereign AI, hyperscaler, enterprise, and industrial customers all in production with Blackwell Ultra
- Software platform (CUDA, NIM, AI Enterprise, NeMo) compounding the architectural moat
- Networking emerging as a second leg of the data center story, growing at ~3x compute growth rate
